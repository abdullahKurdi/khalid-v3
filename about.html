Khalid Mohammed Al-Mutlaq

Master of Traditional Arabic Calligraphy

Khalid Mohammed Al-Mutlaq, born in 1981, is a Saudi artist and calligrapher who holds a certificate in Arabic calligraphy and a professional evaluation certificate from Sotheby's Institute of Art in collaboration with Misk Art Institute. His artistic journey began in 1997 through the Al-Muftaha Village exhibitions and the General Presidency for Youth Welfare through regional exhibitions and club workshops.

He has participated in numerous exhibitions both within and outside the Kingdom, with his works collected by government departments, companies, ministries, and businessmen. In 2022, he won first place in the International Falcons Club competition in Riyadh, and in 2019, he conducted an Arabic calligraphy workshop in London as part of the Ministry of Culture's activities.

Artistic Journey

A life dedicated to the art of the Arabic letter

1981‚Äì1990s

Early Inspiration

Khalid loved art from his early school years, where his eyes would eagerly await the art class to find his teacher writing 'Bismillah al-Rahman al-Raheem' at the top of the blackboard. He would examine the formations of Arabic letters that wove together this beauty in the Basmala, then follow the lesson learning realistic compositions with new enthusiasm through 'Flomaster' colors.

2000s

Al-Muftaha Village Era

With this love, Khalid Al-Mutlaq began his artistic journey by joining Al-Muftaha Village in Asir region in 1997, which became the first station where he mixed with senior visual artists in the Kingdom. Al-Muftaha Village gathered most of the Kingdom's senior artists during the summer period, and this period over 3 years was the stage that led him to professionalism.

2010s

The Birth of Letter Art

In Al-Muftaha Village, alongside the late Abdul Halim Radawi, the first visual artist in Saudi Arabia, during his artistic workshop, Khalid Al-Mutlaq had the experience of introducing letters with other visual elements. The word 'Asir' that penetrated buildings and other elements from the beginning to the end of the work was the beginning of letter art in Khalid Al-Mutlaq's visual works.

2020s

Art Vision Gallery Era

In 2022, artist Khalid Al-Mutlaq returned to active participation with the opening of Art Vision Gallery and began producing diverse artistic masterpieces of his letter works, continuing to this day. Khalid Al-Mutlaq is an artist who loved letters and a calligrapher who loved color and composition, learning calligraphy and mastering it, and professionalizing in visual art to begin a different integration between them.
